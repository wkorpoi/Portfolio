03 : About me

Wadiya M. Korpoi

As a child of West African parents, I was told that showing up as myself and hard work would always earn me success. My family taught me to not be afraid of challenges but to instead take them on head first. These beliefs have always been pivotal to my life. After college, I decided to pursue a career as a flight attendant. However, with the COVID-19 pandemic I was left to explore other options and skill sets. I started an online business where I created my own website and social media marketing, leading me to fall in love with the ability to showcase my creativity through web design. This led me to pivot into a career in software engineering. As a software engineer, I strive to combine my personal values and professional skills to prove that no matter where you are in your journey you can always find your passion.

Prior to this,

I recieved a Bachelors of Science degree in Public Health from West Chester University. I also have a Professional Scrum Master certification. I worked as a Flight Attendant at Delta Airlines, ensuring the safety and comfort of passengers. I also have experience as a direct care staff at a facility for inividuals with intillectual diabilities where I increased independence for individuals in daily living tasks.

Currently,

I'm working as a Software Engineer at Resilient Coders, where I have collaborated with a team of developers to build modern and responsive web applications.
