03 : About me

Wadiya M. Korpoi

Growing up I’ve always had a passion for exploring, whether it be people, new ideas, or new places. I was driven to find a career in exploration, eventually becoming a flight attendant. In 2020 I had to take a step back from my career due to the pandemic and explore other options. I started a business and chose to create my own website and do my own social media marketing. This experience caused me to fall in love with the ability to showcase my creativity and choose to pursue a career in software engineering.

Prior to this,

I worked as a Flight Attendant at Delta Airlines, ensuring the safety and comfort of passengers. They also worked as a Resident Program Worker at Kelsch Associates of PA, where they increased client independence in daily living tasks. I hold a Bachelors of Science degree in Public Health from West Chester University. I also have a Professional Scrum Master certification.

Currently,

I'm working as a Software Engineer at Resilient Coders, where I have collaborated with a team of developers to build modern and responsive web applications.
